# Asana CRM Lead Engagement Workflow

Automate lead engagement by creating, organizing, and tracking CRM tasks in Asana through workflow automation.

---

# 📖 Overview

This project streamlines lead management by automatically creating and organizing tasks in Asana whenever new leads are received or updated.

The workflow ensures that every lead is tracked, assigned, and followed up without requiring manual data entry.

---

# 💼 Business Problem

Sales teams often spend valuable time manually creating tasks, assigning leads, and tracking follow-up activities. As lead volume increases, important opportunities can be missed due to inconsistent processes.

---

# 💡 Solution

Built an automated workflow that:

- Receives new lead information
- Creates corresponding tasks in Asana
- Assigns tasks to the appropriate team member
- Organizes leads into project sections
- Tracks lead progress through the sales pipeline

This automation improves lead visibility, reduces manual work, and helps teams respond to prospects more efficiently.

---

# 🔄 Workflow

```text
New Lead
      │
      ▼
Workflow Trigger
      │
      ▼
Process Lead Information
      │
      ▼
Create Asana Task
      │
      ▼
Assign Team Member
      │
      ▼
Update CRM Pipeline
```
